About

Hi.

I'm Seth. Just another wave in this ocean of tech students. But I want to make a splash. A big one. I wasn't surprised to find that a public university of +40,000 students doesn't really make you feel all that special. So I'm self learning web development skills, honing my programming prowess, and looking for an opportunity.

I want to be a versatile programmer, but I'm really interested in the backend Java and Javascript scene. I'll be your go-to node.js and android guy, making web apps, backend servers and API's. I also believe in the design and aesthetics of our virtual world. I usually have one of two reactions when first experiencing a website/app: A. It's beautiful, how can I totally steal this design for myself?! B. Erm, I think I could make it a little better.

So that's me, I'm a driven computer scientist who enjoys sharing knowledge and teaching. I like making useful things and I feel there is never enough time in the world to do or learn everything on my mental checklist. If you have similar thoughts, maybe we can talk about it!
